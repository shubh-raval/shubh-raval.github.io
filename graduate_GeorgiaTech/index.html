Course Highlights

Course Overview

This course focused on the product design and development process, giving formalized tools to analyze customer needs and evaluate the performance of a product. Some of my favorite have been functional modeling and axiomatic design principles.

Final Project: Design Analysis of Collaborative Robots

An in-depth exploration of functional modeling and axiomatic design principles applied to collaborative robotics, featuring a comprehensive analysis of Universal Robotics' modular cobot design approach.

Course Overview

This course layed the groundwork for optimization engineering. The background of gradient descent, genetic algorithims, neural networks, and other optimization methods were presented. These were then applied for a final project where my group focused on the mutli-objective optimization of a spherical parallel manipulator

Final Project: Multi-Objective Optimization of Spherical Parallel Manipulator

The optimization algorithim aimed to minimize mass while maximizing manipulability for this end effector. Such that it would be suitable to enhance any collaborative robot for dexterous object manipulation while minimizing payload impacts.
